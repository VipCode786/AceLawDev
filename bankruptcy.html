Bankruptcy

The Chambers has a well-rounded practice of both litigation and non-litigation work in the area of Insolvency and Restructuring. Ace Law Chambers advises corporate debtors, nancial and operational creditors, for a range of services which includes:

  • Strategizing and advising the client from the perspective of the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code.
  • Fling/defending insolvency petition before the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) and the National Company Law Appellate Tribunal (NCLAT)
  • Restructuring the corporate debtor and strategizing its revival including
    • Preparation or assisting in examination of resolution plans
    • Initiating/ defending proceeding against guarantors/mortgagors of the corporate debtor
    • Advising and action on behalf of the resolution professional and advising client on the different facets of the insolvency and liquidation process.
  • We also provide advisory on informal restructuring and help in drafting, negotiating and nalizing restructuring plans, transactions documents including Debtor Creditor Agreements, InterCreditor Agreements, Syndicate Loan Agreements, Master Restructuring Agreements etc.
  • We also advise on the enforcement of securities under the Securitisation and Reconstruction of Financial Assets and Enforcement of Security Interest Act, 2002 as per the client's requipxents.
